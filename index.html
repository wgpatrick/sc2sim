⚡ Optimizer — fastest army at the enemy

Pick a target army; the optimizer searches ~2,300 build variants (worker count, production buildings, gas, proxy vs. home) for the one whose army arrives at the enemy soonest.

Costs, supply & build times come from SC2's own game data (sc2-techtree's data.json, current to 5.0.16), in Faster-clock seconds. The income rates are still approximate calibration targets — tune them against headless SC2 to trust exact timings (see the project README).
